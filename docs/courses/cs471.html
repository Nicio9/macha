CS471: Data Science Research Assignment

Semester: 2nd Semester
Prerequisites: CS315

Course Description

The research assignment provides students with a comprehensive learning experience that integrates knowledge from previous courses. Students conduct an independent research project in the area of data science, developing skills in research methodology, critical thinking, and scientific communication.

Learning Outcomes

  • Formulate a research question in data science and develop a plan to address it
  • Conduct an independent literature review
  • Apply and integrate techniques from previous courses to a novel problem
  • Communicate research findings effectively in written and oral form

Assessment

Assessment is based on a research report and oral presentation demonstrating the quality, depth, and communication of the independent research project.
