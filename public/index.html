Welcome to the Computer Science Division

The Department of Computer Science was founded in 1972. We have now merged with Mathematics and Applied Mathematics to form the Computer Science Division in the Department of Mathematical Sciences.

We currently have 24 academic staff members whose research activities span a range of subject areas, including:

Automata Theory & Formal Methods
Machine Learning & AI
Software Engineering
Natural Language Processing
Computer Networks
Robotics & Cognitive Systems
