The Computer Science Division is now accepting applications for Honours and Masters programmes for the 2026 academic year. As part of the Div & Conquer Hackathon 2026, the Computer Science Division has launched a modern, comprehensive website showcasing our academic programmes, research groups, and staff. This new digital presence reflects our commitment to excellence in computer science education and research at Stellenbosch University.

New Research Collaboration: Machine Learning for Earth Observation

The Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence research group has established a collaboration with international partners on earth observation and remote sensing applications. This project combines machine learning expertise with geographic information systems for environmental monitoring and climate research.
