Academic Programmes

The Computer Science Division at Stellenbosch University offers comprehensive undergraduate and postgraduate programmes designed to prepare students for careers in computing and research. Our flexible structure allows students to specialize in areas such as data science, software engineering, machine learning, and more.

Undergraduate Programmes

Undergraduate

BSc Computer Science

Duration: 3 years

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science is a flexible degree that allows students to choose focal areas aligned with their career interests. All students complete a core curriculum covering fundamental computing concepts, algorithms, data structures, and systems, while having the freedom to specialize.

Entry Requirements:
  • Minimum NSC Level 5 or equivalent in Mathematics
  • English language proficiency
  • Competitive admission based on academic performance
Available Focal Areas:

General Computer Science

Broad training in computing with flexibility to choose Science modules, ideal for those interested in diverse computing topics.

Computer Systems

CS major combined with Electrical & Electronic Engineering modules for systems-focused careers.

Data Science

CS major supplemented with Statistics and Data Science modules for careers in analytics and data-driven problem solving.

CS with Genetics (Second Major)

Combined major programme integrating computational biology and bioinformatics.

CS with Geographical Information Technology

Combined major programme for GIS and spatial data applications.

Core Curriculum (All Focal Areas):
  • Year 1: Introductory Computer Science 1 & 2
  • Year 2: Data Structures & Algorithms, Computer Architecture, plus electives
  • Year 3: Computer Networks, Machine Learning, Databases & Web Programming, plus advanced topics in automata, concurrency, and program design
Undergraduate

BDatSci Bachelor of Data Science

Duration: 4 years

The Bachelor of Data Science is a specialized 4-year degree designed for students passionate about extracting insights from data. This programme combines Computer Science courses with advanced Mathematics and Statistics, preparing graduates for data science, analytics, and research careers.

Entry Requirements:
  • Strong background in Mathematics and Sciences
  • Minimum NSC Level 5 in Mathematics
  • Quantitative aptitude and analytical thinking
Focal Area Options:

Computer Science Track

Full CS curriculum with data science focus

Applied Mathematics

Mathematical foundations for data science

Statistical Physics

Physics-based approaches to modeling complex systems

Analytics & Optimization

Quantitative methods for decision-making

Behavioral Economics

Economic and behavioral dimensions of data analysis

Statistical Learning

Statistical foundations of machine learning

Postgraduate Programmes

Honours

Honours in Computer Science (CS797)

Duration: 1 year (full-time) or 2 years (part-time)

The Honours degree is an intensive postgraduate programme designed to deepen expertise in Computer Science. Students complete 6 elective modules (96 credits) and an independent honours project (32 credits) under supervision.

Entry Requirements:
  • 3-year BSc degree or equivalent
  • At least 32 credits at 2nd/3rd year level in Computer Science or related quantitative disciplines
  • Competitive selection process
  • Mandatory full-time attendance (if selecting full-time option)
Programme Structure:
  • 6 elective modules (choose based on interests)
  • Honours project (large software construction or research problem)
  • Independent supervision by faculty members
  • Specialization options: Machine Learning, Data Science, Software Engineering, and more
Masters

Masters in Computer Science (CS878)

Duration: 2 years (typical)

The Master's degree is a research-focused programme requiring students to conduct original research under faculty supervision. Students develop a significant contribution to their field of study through independent research leading to a thesis.

Entry Requirements:
  • Honours degree in Computer Science or equivalent
  • Strong academic record
  • Research proposal (to be developed with supervisor)
  • Faculty supervisor assignment based on research interests
Key Features:
  • Independent research on approved topic
  • Regular supervision meetings
  • Progress reports and milestones
  • Final thesis submission and evaluation
  • Flexibility in research timeline and specialization
PhD

PhD in Computer Science (CS978)

Duration: 3+ years

The PhD programme is designed for students committed to making significant contributions to Computer Science through original research. This is a comprehensive research degree requiring development of new knowledge in the field.

Entry Requirements:
  • Master's degree or Honours degree with strong research record
  • Clear research interests and goals
  • Faculty supervisor willing to supervise research topic
  • Research proposal development
Key Features:
  • Comprehensive original research
  • Supervision by experienced faculty
  • Progress reviews and evaluations
  • Final dissertation and defense
  • Opportunity to contribute to academic conferences and publications
  • Flexible timeline based on research needs
