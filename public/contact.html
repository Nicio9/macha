Visiting Campus

Getting to Stellenbosch University

By Air: Cape Town International Airport is approximately 50km from Stellenbosch (45-60 minutes by car).

By Road: The university is conveniently located on the N1 highway between Cape Town and Franschhoek. Free parking is available on campus.

Public Transport: Long-distance buses and shuttle services operate between Cape Town and Stellenbosch.

Campus Tours: For visiting information and campus tours, please contact the main university website or call the main switchboard at +27 21 808 9111.
