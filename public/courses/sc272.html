SC272: Scientific Computing

Semester: 1st-2nd Semester
Prerequisites: None

Course Description

This course covers advanced topics in computer science. For detailed information, please visit the main course catalogue.

Learning Outcomes

  • Develop advanced technical skills in the course subject area
  • Apply theoretical concepts to practical problems
  • Participate in hands-on projects and assignments

Assessment

Assessment is based on practical assignments, examinations, and projects that demonstrate mastery of the course concepts.
